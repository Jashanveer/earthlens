Description:
EarthLens brings striking Earth View satellite imagery to your Mac desktop.

Browse a rotating collection of scenic satellite wallpapers, change the current wallpaper with one click, or let EarthLens refresh your desktop automatically in the background. The app lives quietly in the menu bar, opens a focused wallpaper preview when you need it, and returns to the menu bar when you close the window.

Features:
- Set a new Earth View wallpaper with one click
- Step back to previous wallpapers from your current history
- Auto-rotate wallpapers every 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 1 hour, or 3 hours
- Start at login when Auto-Rotate is enabled
- Cache the catalog and downloaded wallpapers locally
- No account, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking

EarthLens downloads public Earth View catalog data and wallpaper images directly to your Mac. App state and cached images stay on your device.
